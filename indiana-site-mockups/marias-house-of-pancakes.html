💛 Our Story

A Home at the Table

Maria's House of Pancakes has been serving the North Webster community for years, welcoming locals and lake visitors alike with the kind of breakfast that makes you want to linger a little longer over your coffee.

Every pancake is flipped by hand, every egg cracked fresh, and every cup of coffee poured with care. We're not just a restaurant — we're the place where families celebrate birthdays,.friends catch up on weekends, and everybody knows your name.

Our kitchen uses real butter, real maple syrup, and recipes that have been passed down through generations. When you walk through our doors, you're family.

— Maria & The Family
