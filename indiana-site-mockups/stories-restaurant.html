Our Story

A Greensburg Tradition Since 1977

For nearly five decades, Storie's Restaurant has been the place where Greensburg gathers. What started as a small kitchen on East Main Street became a community cornerstone — known for hand-breaded tenderloins, scratch-made soups, and pies that taste like home.

Our kitchen still does things the old-fashioned way: fresh ingredients, generous portions, and recipes that haven't changed since the beginning. And for something a little different, keep an eye out for our occasional Indonesian specialty meals — a nod to family roots that make Storie's one of a kind.

— The Storie's Family
