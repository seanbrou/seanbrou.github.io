Our Story

A Family Tradition of Crazy Good Food

Creighton's Crazy Egg Cafe & Coffee Bar was born from a simple idea: breakfast should be the best meal of the day, not just the first one. What started as a dream in the Creighton family kitchen has grown into one of Warsaw's most loved gathering spots.

We believe in the power of a perfectly scrambled egg, a well-pulled espresso, and a place where everybody knows your name — or at least your usual order. Every dish is made from scratch, using ingredients from local Indiana farms and purveyors we trust.

Whether you're a regular or it's your first time through the door, we treat every morning like a reason to celebrate. Pull up a chair, stay a while, and see why they call us crazy — crazy about breakfast, that is.
