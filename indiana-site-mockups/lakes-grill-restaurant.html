Our Story

Family Recipes,
Neighborly Love

Lakes Grill was born from a simple idea: a place where hardworking folks can sit down to an honest meal at a fair price. Tucked along State Road 15 in the heart of Silver Lake, we've been dishing out generous portions of comfort food and warm conversation since we first turned on the grill.

Whether you're a local pulling in for the usual or a traveler just passing through, we treat everyone like family. That's the Lakes Grill promise — good food, good people, and always a seat at the table.
