Our Story

Baked with Purpose

Cornerstone Bakery Bistro Coffee was born from a simple belief: that a neighborhood deserves a place where every detail — from the flour we choose to the way we pour your coffee — is handled with intention and heart.

For years, we've been Greensburg's morning ritual and midday retreat. Our bakers arrive before dawn so the aroma of fresh bread greets you when you walk through the door. Our baristas know your name, your order, and how your day's been.

"We're not just a bakery. We're the corner of Greensburg where community rises — one loaf, one cup, one conversation at a time."
