★ Root Beer Heritage

A Greensburg Tradition
Worth Pulling Over For

Since the day the Fry family fired up the grill and poured the first frosty mug, this little drive-in on North Lincoln Street has been more than a restaurant — it's been a gathering place. A first-date spot. A summer night ritual. The place where kids who once sat in the backseat now bring kids of their own.

That unmistakable A&W root beer flavor hasn't changed, and neither has the feeling: roll down the window, place your order, and watch the world slow down for a minute.
