A Granbury Family Tradition

For years, A-One Donuts & Bakery has been the heart and soul of Granbury's mornings. What started as a dream to bring fresh, handcrafted donuts to our community has grown into a beloved daily ritual for hundreds of local families.

Every morning — before the sun is fully up — our team is already at work, baking fresh donuts and pastries from scratch using quality ingredients. We believe in the simple magic of a warm donut, a friendly smile, and a community that shows up for each other.

"Every donut is made with love — because you're family."
