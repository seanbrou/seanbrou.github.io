Our Story

Real Home Cooking,
For Any Event Size.

J & J started with a simple problem: someone needed to feed 40 people for a party and couldn't find catering that actually felt like home cooking. That problem turned into a catering business and a food truck that shows up wherever the crowds are in and around Indianapolis. Every dish is made from scratch with ingredients you'd recognize at the store.
