House Favorites

The Brazos Breakfast

Two eggs any style, smoked sausage links, crispy bacon, hash browns, and toast. The plate that keeps 'em coming back.

Chicken Fried Steak

Pancake Stack

A Tin Top Tradition Since Day One

Mary's Brazos Cafe has been the heart of Tin Top's mornings for years. When you walk through our doors, you're not just a customer — you're family.

We believe in good food, honest portions, and the kind of unpretentious service that makes you feel right at home. Our regulars know the drill: cup of coffee's already poured, and your usual is ready when you are.

From our hearty breakfast plates to our soul-warming comfort classics, every dish is made to order with the stuff that matters — quality ingredients and a whole lot of care.
