General outreach angle
- Lead with the fact that you already made something specific for them.
- Keep it low-pressure.
- Make the value concrete: better first impression, easier ordering/contact, more trust, more direct calls/messages.
- Ask for a tiny next step: “If you want, I can customize this and make it live for you.”
- Avoid sounding like a mass template.
