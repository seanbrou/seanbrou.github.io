Our Story

The Midwest's Tropical Escape

Step through our doors and Greensburg fades away. The flickering torches, open-air patio, and island rhythms are your passport to somewhere warmer. The Beach Tiki Bar & Grill started as a dream — bring the beach to Indiana — and became the town's favorite night out.

From signature volcano bowls to our famous Fish Tacos, every drink and dish is made to remind you that the best escape is sometimes the closest one.
