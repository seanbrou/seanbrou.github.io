Our Story

Maria's House of Pancakes has been serving the North Webster community with love since day one. Born from Maria's passion for bringing families together around the breakfast table, every dish is made from scratch with the finest ingredients. We believe breakfast is more than a meal — it's a warm start to a great day. Come pull up a chair and let us take care of the rest.
