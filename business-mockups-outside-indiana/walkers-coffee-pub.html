Our Story

A Gathering Place
Rooted in Athens

Walker's Coffee & Pub opened its doors in 2008 on a quiet corner of downtown Athens, GA — a town already buzzing with music, art, and the kind of energy that doesn't sleep. We wanted to build a place that captured that spirit: somewhere you could start your morning with a proper espresso or finish your night with a pint and a plate of nachos.

Sixteen years later, we're still family-owned and still doing it the same way — fresh food, local draft beers, coffee roasted in small batches, and a staff that actually remembers your name. Whether you're here for a study session, a first date, or just somewhere warm to sit and think, our door is open.
