Our Story

The Grove began with a simple dream: bring the authentic taste of wood-fired pizza to Granbury. Our clay oven, fired to 900 degrees, creates that perfect char on our hand-stretched dough.

Every pizza starts with flour milled in small batches, tomatoes crushed fresh, and cheeses aged to perfection. We source from local Texas farms whenever possible, because great pizza starts with great ingredients.

Whether you're here for a romantic evening, family gathering, or just a craving worth satisfying, you'll taste the difference that passion makes.
