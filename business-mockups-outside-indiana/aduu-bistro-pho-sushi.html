Our Story

Aduu Bistro Pho & Sushi

Aduu Bistro was born from a love of gathering around food that feels like home — whether that means a steaming bowl of pho after a long day or perfectly crafted sushi shared with friends.

We bring together two culinary traditions under one roof in Granbury, Texas. Every broth is simmered for hours. Every roll is made to order. We believe great food should be both an art and a comfort.

— The Aduu Bistro Family
