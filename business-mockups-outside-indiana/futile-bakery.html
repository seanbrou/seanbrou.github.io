About Futile

Baked in small batches, by hand, in Lexington.

We opened Futile because we believe mornings deserve better than a microwave danish. Every dough is mixed from scratch; every batch is sized small so nothing sits.

  • Everything from scratch. No pre-made dough, no cake mix shortcuts. If it came from our oven, it started as flour and butter.
  • Small batch, every day. We bake multiple times throughout the morning so the case stays fresh and the cinnamon rolls are warm when you arrive.
  • Gift boxes ship statewide. Cookie tins and seasonal boxes go out with a week lead time. Email to arrange.
  • Morning-first hours. We open early and we close when the case sells out — which is the honest way to run a bakery.
