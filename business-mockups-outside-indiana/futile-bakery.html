About Futile

Baked in small batches, by hand, in Lexington.

We opened Futile because mornings deserve better than a microwave danish. Every dough is mixed from scratch; every batch is sized small so nothing sits.

Everything from scratch

No pre-made dough, no cake mix shortcuts. If it came from our oven, it started as flour and butter.

Small batch, every day
